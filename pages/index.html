About Us

We are a group of high school students from the Center for Information Technology at Deep Run High School. In the past, the method for storing and sharing vocational certifications have proven to be risky and ineffective. Paper certifications can easily be lost or damaged and they are often difficult to share with prospective employers. Keeping in mind the obstacles throughout this outdated approach, we have been tasked with creating a hub for storing vocational job certifications and offering training. Our users are able to create their own unique profile with their current field of study and their existing certifications. Not only does our solution eliminate the risk associated with physical copies, but it also allows our users to consolidate and organize their certifications. Our website also provides links to vocational certification courses to allow users to obtain more certificates through professional courses. We hope to improve the feasibility surrounding vocational employment and training.
